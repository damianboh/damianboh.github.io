Welcome

Simulations are catered to topics in the GCE O-Level Physics syllabus.

Simulations

Where applicable, auto-generated step-by-step methods to arrive at certain solutions as well as the explanations are given for a variety of input scenarios and initial variables in the simulations. Accompanying the visual animations are descriptions of physical phenomena involved in the simulations to aid students in describing physical phenomena and obsevations in words.
